8.4. List stuff

Itemized list - works in LaTeX and Docbook modes. An example of the itemized list:

  • Scorn

  • Nirvana

  • Guano Apes

Enumerated list - works in LaTeX (as enumerated list) and Docbook (as orderedlist) modes. An example of the enumerated list:

  1. Scorn

  2. Nirvana

  3. Guano Apes
