Chapter 16. About the author

Realname: Peter Semiletov, nick: Roxton (reversed XOR and NOT operators); was born in Kiev (USSR), at 1977. Currently I work as a journalist writing mostly about Free Software/Open Source and digital sound/video processing. Apart from my daily job, I write novels and stories, make music using virtual synths and live guitar. Also I'm a drummer in the punk/folk band Kakayato (it can be translated like as "someone"),and singer/guitarist in avantgarde band Kostniy Mozg. I am a vegetarian (due to ethical reason) and an atheist. My home site is here
